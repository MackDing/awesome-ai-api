## Pricing

OpenRouter passes through official pricing with a small margin (typically 0–10%). Different providers for the same model are listed side-by-side; users can choose by price, latency, or uptime.

See live pricing at [openrouter.ai/models](https://openrouter.ai/models).

- 💳 Credit card (Stripe)
- ₿ Crypto (USDC via Coinbase)
- ❌ No Alipay/WeChat

## Features

- **Multi-provider routing** — Same model via different backends; auto-failover
- **Per-request provider selection** — Pin to a specific provider when needed
- **Analytics dashboard** — Token spend, latency, error rates per key
- **OAuth PKCE flow** for third-party app integration
- **Free tier** on several models (rate-limited)

## Pros & Cons

**Pros**
- Widest model catalog of any gateway
- Transparent pricing, side-by-side provider comparison
- Strong developer tooling and docs
- Active on Twitter/Discord for support

**Cons**
- No direct Alipay/WeChat — hard for Chinese users without a card
- Margin means no significant price discount vs. official
- Enterprise features (SLA, invoicing) less mature than dedicated resellers
