## Pros & Cons

**Pros**
- CN payment built in (Alipay/WeChat)
- Wide coverage of latest models
- Active operator community

**Cons**
- Primarily Chinese-language UI/docs
- Limited public transparency on uptime / SLA
- Smaller user base than OpenRouter — fewer third-party reviews
