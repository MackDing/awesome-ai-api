# Blacklist | 黑名单

Gateways flagged for **serious**, **verified** issues. Listing here requires multiple independent reports and public evidence.

被标注为**严重**并经**验证**的中转站。上榜需多条独立举报 + 公开证据。

## Current Entries | 当前条目

*No entries yet.* | *暂无条目。*

## How an entry gets here | 如何被列入

1. At least 2 independent reports in [Discussions](https://github.com/MackDing/awesome-ai-api/discussions) under `Reports` category
2. Evidence archive added to this repo under `data/blacklist-evidence/<slug>/`
3. Gateway operator has 14 days to respond in the same discussion
4. Maintainer merges the PR after review

至少 2 条独立举报 + 证据存档 + 给对方 14 天答辩期 + 维护者审核后合并 PR。

## Removal | 移除

Entries can be removed if the issue is resolved, the business has verifiably changed ownership, or the original evidence is retracted.

问题解决、业务探明换主、或证据被撤回的条目可移除。

## Good-faith disclosure | 善意声明

This blacklist is a **factual record of complaints**, not defamation. We link to public evidence and allow operators to respond.

本黑名单是**投诉事实记录**,非诽谤。所有条目均有公开证据并允许当事方答辩。
